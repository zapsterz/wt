What we do

Waggy Tails is a club for young people with special needs/disabilities. We have specially trained dogs that attend, giving our members the opportunity to interact with them and bring about confidence through responsibility.

Who we are here for

The Waggy Tails members are aged from 13-18 years. All of our members have special needs/disabilities, with social and communication difficulties being the common link between them. We never set out to distinguish which disabilities Waggy Tails would best suit. It just happened that quite a few of our children at Waggy Tails are on the Autistic Spectrum e.g. Aspergers Syndrome.

What goes on

Waggy Tails is divided up into different activity rooms for our members to enjoy. The main activities are Obedience Training and Dog Tricks and Games.
