At Waggy Tails there are many areas of fun!

Dog Obedience Room

The Obedience Training sessions run from 4:30-5pm, 5-5:30pm, 5:30-6pm, and 6-6:30pm. Karen and Hayley put the Waggy Tails members and their dogs through their training. Here they will teach their dogs to sit, stay, lie down, come and walk to heel.

Trick and Puzzle Room

The dogs in the trick room enjoy doing tricks and solving problems. There are also games for the Waggy Tails members to play.

Agility Room

We have dog jumps, tunnels and weaves for training the dogs with the agility equipment. The dogs in this room are very good at obstacle courses.

Vet check and Reading Room

The dogs in here are very calm, and enjoy being read to. This room is brilliant for Waggy Tails members who enjoy a calm and quiet environment. The vet check is good for getting to know how to stroke the dogs and treat them with care.

Parent Zone

There is a parent zone at Waggy Tails with tea and coffee. This provides an opportunity for some relaxation and a chance to meet people in similar situations and circumstances.
